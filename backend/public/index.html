Identify insights for your building

Rate your building's property value, environmental impact, transit score, and safeness

Endpoint
GET/insight
Query Parameters
heightString

The height of your building

latString

The latitude of your building's location

lengthString

The length of your building

lngString

The longitude of your building's location

occupantsString

The number of occupants that will use this building

squareFootageString

The square footage of your building

typeString

The type of building (Office Building, Family, Retail Store, Residential, Commercial, Office/Industrial, Public, Mixed Use, Miscellaneous)

useApisString

Whether or not to use APIs (1 for yes, 0 for no. Disable to save API Calls!)

widthString

The width of your building
